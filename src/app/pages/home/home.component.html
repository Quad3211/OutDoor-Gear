About Island Adventure Gear

Island Adventure Gear is Jamaica's premier outdoor equipment store. We provide high-quality gear for hikers, campers, and nature enthusiasts across the island. Whether you're exploring the Blue Mountains or planning a beach camping trip in Negril, we have everything you need.

Founded in 2018 in Kingston, we started with a passion for Jamaica's stunning natural landscapes. Today we serve hundreds of adventure seekers each month, proud to be the local brand that knows the terrain.
